How does deforestation lead to frequent floods and droughts?


Due to deforestation, the water holding capacity of the soil decreases. The roots of the trees get dried after they're cut down. The movement of water through trees into the soil slows down. The water of the rainfall collects on the surface which results into heavy flood. so it is high to stop cutting down tree to make our world natural climate free.
